# 🏡 Sakarya.City

**Sakarya.City** is a modern real estate platform dedicated exclusively to Sakarya, Türkiye.

The platform connects property buyers, sellers, investors, and real estate professionals through a fast, reliable, and user-friendly marketplace.

## Features

- 🏠 Apartments for Sale
- 🏡 Apartments for Rent
- 🌿 Land Listings
- 🏘️ Villas
- 🏢 Commercial Properties
- 🏗️ New Housing Projects
- 🏢 Real Estate Agencies
- 📍 District-Based Property Search
- 📱 Mobile-Friendly Platform

## Coverage

The platform serves every district of Sakarya, including:

- Adapazarı
- Serdivan
- Arifiye
- Erenler
- Sapanca
- Akyazı
- Hendek
- Karasu
- Kocaali
- Kaynarca
- Geyve
- Pamukova
- Ferizli
- Söğütlü
- Karapürçek
- Taraklı

## Our Mission

To provide a trusted and easy-to-use digital marketplace where buyers, sellers, investors, and real estate professionals can connect across Sakarya.
